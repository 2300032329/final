Grilled Chicken Salad Recipe

Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup toasted nuts (almonds, walnuts)
  • Olive oil, for grilling
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Brush the chicken breasts with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked (internal temperature should reach 165°F). Once done, let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, avocado, and toasted nuts.
  4. Slice the grilled chicken and add it to the salad.
  5. Top with crumbled feta cheese (optional).
  6. Drizzle your favorite dressing over the salad and toss lightly to combine.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Chef's Tips:

  • If you don't have a grill, you can use a grill pan or a regular pan to cook the chicken.
  • For a tangy twist, try using a balsamic vinaigrette or lemon dressing.
  • You can substitute feta cheese with goat cheese or skip the cheese entirely for a dairy-free option.
  • To save time, use pre-cooked or rotisserie chicken.
