Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt, for pasta water
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, in a pan over medium heat, cook the pancetta until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated cheese, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  4. Once the spaghetti is cooked, reserve a cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  5. Immediately add the hot spaghetti to the pan with pancetta and toss to combine.
  6. Remove the pan from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture, adding a little reserved pasta water if necessary to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with additional cheese, black pepper, and chopped parsley.

Chef's Tips:

  • Use high-quality ingredients for the best flavor, especially the cheese and cured meat.
  • Work quickly when adding the egg mixture to avoid scrambling the eggs.
  • If you prefer a richer sauce, you can add an additional egg yolk.
  • Serve with a simple green salad for a complete meal.
