Avocado Toast Recipe

Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 4 slices of bread (sourdough or whole grain)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (like cilantro or basil, optional)
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved (optional)
  • Olive oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices to your desired crispiness.
  2. While the bread is toasting, cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the avocados. Mash with a fork until you reach your desired consistency.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado generously over the toasted bread.
  5. Top with red pepper flakes, fresh herbs, cherry tomatoes, and a drizzle of olive oil if desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious avocado toast!

Chef's Tips:

  • For extra creaminess, try adding a bit of Greek yogurt or cream cheese to the avocado mash.
  • Experiment with different toppings like radishes, poached eggs, or smoked salmon.
  • Choose ripe avocados for the best flavor and texture.
  • Avocado toast is best enjoyed fresh, but leftovers can be stored in the fridge for a few hours.
